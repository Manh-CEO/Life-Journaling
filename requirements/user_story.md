## Epic 1: Automated Engagement (The "Push" System)

*This epic covers how the system initiates contact to gather data without the user needing to open the app.*

* **US1: Weekly Check-in Prompt**
* **As a** user, **I want** to receive a weekly email with a reflective question **so that** I can record my life events by simply replying to a message.


* **US2: Annual Portrait Request**
* **As a** user, **I want** the system to ask for a photo on my "Anchor Date" (e.g., birthday) **so that** I can track my physical changes over the years.


* **US13: Missed Check-in Handling**
* **As a** user, **I want** the system to send one gentle reminder if I haven't replied **so that** I don't leave gaps in my story.



---

## Epic 2: Data Intelligence & Processing

*The backend "magic" that turns messy text into structured memories.*

* **US3: Event Extraction (AI Parsing)**
* **As a** system, **I want** to use AI to parse email replies **so that** I can automatically identify dates, locations, and key events.


* **US4: Sentiment Tagging**
* **As a** user, **I want** my entries to be automatically tagged with a mood **so that** I can visualize my emotional journey over time.



---

## Epic 3: User Experience & Profile (The "Home")

*What the user sees when they actually open the mobile application.*

* **US15: Personal Identity Dashboard (Initial Screen)**
* **As a** user, **I want** to see my profile, current age/milestone, and my most recent yearly photo immediately upon opening the app **so that** I feel a personal connection to my data.


* **US5: Visual Timeline**
* **As a** user, **I want** an interactive vertical timeline of all my recorded events **so that** I can scroll through my past easily.


* **US6: Memory Retrieval (Search)**
* **As a** user, **I want** to search my memories using natural language (e.g., "When was the last time I felt happy?") **so that** I can find specific moments quickly.



---

## Epic 4: Manual Control & Management

*Giving the user the power to override the automation.*

* **US11: Manual Log Entry**
* **As a** user, **I want** to add an event directly in the app at any time **so that** I don't have to wait for the weekly email to record something important.


* **US12: Edit & Delete Operations**
* **As a** user, **I want** to correct AI mistakes or delete entries **so that** my timeline remains accurate and private.


* **US14: Historical Photo Upload**
* **As a** user, **I want** to upload a portrait from my gallery if I missed the "live" photo request **so that** my yearly collection is complete.



---

## Epic 5: System Configuration & Onboarding

*Setting the foundation for each user.*

* **US9: Context Setup**
* **As a** new user, **I want** to define my timezone and "Anchor Date" **so that** the system triggers work at the right time for me.


* **US10: Schedule Preferences**
* **As a** user, **I want** to choose which day of the week I get questioned **so that** the app fits into my routine.



---

## Epic 6: Security & Data Legacy

*Protecting the data for the long term.*

* **US7: Privacy & Encryption**
* **As a** user, **I want** my data to be encrypted **so that** my personal reflections remain confidential and secure.


* **US8: Legacy Export**
* **As a** user, **I want** to export my entire life story into a PDF or digital book **so that** I can keep a permanent copy or share it with family.



---

### Implementation Summary

* **Core USP:** US1, US2, US3, US15.
* **Total Stories:** 15.
* **Primary User Interface:** Mobile App (Profile & Timeline).
* **Primary Interaction:** Email (Inbound parsing).
